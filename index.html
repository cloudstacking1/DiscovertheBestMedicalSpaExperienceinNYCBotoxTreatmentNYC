What is a Medical Spa?

A medical spa, often called a medspa or medi-spa, blends the therapeutic services of a spa with the clinical expertise of medical professionals. Unlike traditional spas, medical spas provide treatments that require medical knowledge and supervision, such as Botox and dermal fillers. These services go beyond relaxation, targeting the root causes of aging and skin imperfections to enhance your natural beauty safely and effectively.
